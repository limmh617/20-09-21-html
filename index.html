Welcome to the
Saehan Math Studio

Saehan Math Studio is operating to answer the creative
needs of students majoring in design by providing workspaces,
equipments, community.

새한수학작업실은 작업공간, 장비, 커뮤니티 등을 제공해,

INFO
Started in 2019

Saehan Math Studio, which has been located in Gongneung since 2019,
was started by 6 students who had overlapping demands for work space.
Now we have more members, 7 VD, 1 ID, and 1 snail friend.

2019년 공릉에 자리 잡은 새한수학작업실은 작업공간에 대한 수요가 겹친
6명의 학생이 시작했습니다. 현재는 구성원이 늘어 VD 7명, ID 1명, 달팽이 한마리로
구성 중입다.
